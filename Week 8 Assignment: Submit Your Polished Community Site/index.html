Privacy & Data Controls

We respect your privacy. All data is stored locally on your device, not on our servers.

  • What we store: Volunteer form information (name/email), resource filter preferences, and visit count.
  • Why we store it: To make the site easier to use by remembering your preferences and draft forms.
  • Where it's stored: On your device only using your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server.
  • How long we keep it: Form data expires after 14 days, filter preferences after 7 days, automatically.
  • Your controls: Use the checkbox in the volunteer form to turn saving on/off. Use "Clear saved data" button to delete immediately.
  • External data: This website does not collect, track, or send any data to external servers.

Note: For actual volunteer sign-ups or delivery requests, please use our official Linktree link which may have its own privacy policy.
