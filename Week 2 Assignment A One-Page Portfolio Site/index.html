About

Hi, I’m Daniel — a Creative Technology & Design (ATLS) student in CU Boulder’s College of Engineering & Applied Science. I co-founded Hot Beam Productions, a full-service production company that designs and runs lights, lasers, and stage systems for live shows. I’m especially curious about how AI can assist real-time show control, AV systems design, and smarter workflows for design and documentation.

Recently, I’ve been experimenting with music analysis for lighting automation, Q-SYS control scripting, and hardware prototypes that make data more tangible. I like projects that blend engineering, creativity, and live experiences.

Interests

  • AI for creative tooling and real-time show control
  • AV systems (Q-SYS programming, routing, and control UIs)
  • Lighting & laser design for events and installations
  • Human-centered tools that automate boring parts of documentation
  • Hardware tinkering (microcontrollers, small displays, sensors)

Goals

Academic

  • Deepen my foundation in data structures, systems, and HCI.
  • Document projects clearly so others can build on them.

Professional

  • Ship reliable tools that improve AV design and live production workflows.
  • Grow Hot Beam Productions with more sophisticated, safe, and scalable shows.

Personal

  • Balance engineering focus with creative exploration.
  • Collaborate with people who love building useful things.
