Privacy & Controls

Short version: We only store what you choose to share in this browser to make the site easier to use. You can opt out and clear everything at any time.

  • What we store: Volunteer form name/email (if you opt in) and your resource filters.
  • Why: So your draft form and filters are here next time.
  • Where: On your device using your browser’s localStorage. Nothing is sent to a server from this demo.
  • How long: Form data auto-expires after 14 days; filters after 7 days.
  • Controls: Use the checkbox in the form to turn saving on/off, and the “Clear saved data” button to delete immediately.
  • External data: This demo doesn’t fetch external data.
